I’m Steven Dao, a programmer and technical artist interested in 3D graphics and animation. I’ve worked on projects ranging from video games to motion capture.

At the moment, I live in the San Francisco Bay Area. I work on Android at Google in Mountain View, CA.

Originally from Texas, I graduated from The University of Texas at Austin in May 2015 with a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy and Computer Science and a Game Development Certificate. I previously interned at DreamWorks Animation in Glendale, California and at Google’s Cambridge, Massachusetts, office.
